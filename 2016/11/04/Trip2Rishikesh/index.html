Trip2Rishikesh

4 min. read

Nov 04, 2016 in Travel

I went to Rishikesh from October 30 to November 3, 2016.

Rishikesh known as “the valley of saints” is an amazing place for both adventurers as well as people in search of Spiritual calling.This Blog post is about the former.

Table of Contents

  • How to reach ?
  • Riverside Night Camping
  • River Rafting
  • Bungee Jumping
  • Beatles Ashram
  • Others

The Trip

How to Reach?

To reach Rishikesh you have to first reach Haridwar or Dehradun. We reached Haridwar by train after realizing there is nothing much to do there we took a bus to Rishikesh. Which took almost 1.25 hours and is available 24 * 7 . You can also get Taxi’s or Auto’s.

Camping

After reaching we took a river side camp in Shivpuri near Rishikesh for the nights stay. The package included food,camping and free rafting on the next day.Food was great and nothing can ever match the ambiance of the atmosphere though. The Bon fire with with the cold winds from the Himalayas are something worth experiencing.

We had to wake up early in the morning to go for river rafting.

Rafting

River Rafting is one of the cheapest and most amazing things to do in Rishikesh.
We did a 18 km rafting which included few grade 3 and grade 4 Rapids. Being at the front of the raft i had an interesting journey.
During rafting they allow us to jump into River Ganges and do body floating(with their life jackets on). Which is basically the only time non swimmers can actually experience the beauty of the sport without putting in any effort(P.S i can swim). We stopped half way during the ride for a Tea break where people could do Cliff jumping , which is basically people jumping from a rock into the water. After which we rowed to the end point and finished our ride.

Bungee

The main purpose of this trip was to do Bungee Jumping at Jumpin heights which is an 83 meters long India’s highest jump with jump masters from New Zealand .
It costs around Rs 3600 for a jump which is same everywhere , so you dont have to roam around finding people to bargain with. It is of top quality and is worth every single penny of it. The Jumping place is around 20 km away from Rishikesh which rented a bike for the travel.

After the jump we have to do a small trek up the mountain we just jumped . The place also has Flying Fox and Giant Swing.

BeatlesAshram

Beatles Ashram is the place where the infamous Beatles stayed when they came to India. This is place is full of Graffiti and paintings which will make the artist within you fall in love.

Well that was a lot wasn’t it. There are more but i got tired of clicking pics. There are small caves and very old ashrams there also.

Others

There are many more interesting places like Ram Jhula, Lakshman Jhula , Triveni Ghat and many temples in Rishikesh too.But this post wont cover them.

Thank you for reading and i hope one day you will also visit there and try out everything.
