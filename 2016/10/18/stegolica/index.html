Stegolica

5 min. read

Oct 18, 2016 in Events and activities

Stegolica was held as part of Effervesence 2016 of IIIT Allahabad.

Steganography is the practice of concealing messages or information within other non-secret text or data.

This blog post contains all the questions and their solutions for the event.
